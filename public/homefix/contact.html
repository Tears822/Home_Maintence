FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions – Contact & Support

Quick answers to common questions about reaching the HomeFix team.

  • How do I log an urgent maintenance issue?

    Call 800 626826 or send a WhatsApp message to +971 800 626826 with "URGENT" in the first line. Our coordinator will prioritise the request and confirm attendance windows.

  • Can I book multiple services in one request?

    Yes. List everything you need in the form or WhatsApp message. We will group the jobs, align technician skills, and confirm a consolidated visit.

  • Do you support annual maintenance contract clients separately?

    AMC customers receive priority routing and a dedicated coordinator. Provide your AMC details when you contact us and we will handle the request under your plan.

  • What information helps you schedule faster?

    Share access instructions, parking info, tenancy status, preferred timings, and any previous case references. Photos or short videos help technicians prepare the right tools and materials.
