  • What types of properties does HomeFix service in Dubai?

    HomeFix provides maintenance services for apartments, villas, offices, retail spaces, and mixed-use commercial properties across Dubai.

  • How is HomeFix different from regular maintenance companies?

    HomeFix combines scheduled preventive maintenance, emergency response, and a 24/7 helpdesk under one accountable system with transparent updates and guaranteed workmanship.

  • Do you offer both one-time services and annual maintenance contracts?

    Yes. You can book one-time maintenance services or choose an AMC plan based on your property type, support priority, and servicing frequency requirements.

  • How quickly can HomeFix respond to urgent maintenance issues?

    Emergency response depends on your selected package, with faster commitments in higher AMC tiers. Our support desk is available 24/7 to dispatch urgent jobs quickly.

  • Are your technicians trained to handle different systems and property types?

    Yes. Our technicians are trained across AC, electrical, plumbing, and general maintenance systems for both residential and commercial environments.

  • Can HomeFix manage multiple services under a single contract?

    Absolutely. HomeFix can bundle AC, electrical, plumbing, and additional maintenance scopes into one AMC framework with centralized tracking and support.

    You can book via WhatsApp, call 800 626826, or submit a request form. Our team responds quickly to confirm scope, timing, and dispatch.
