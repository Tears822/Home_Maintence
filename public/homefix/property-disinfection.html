Why Disinfection Matters in Dubai

Cleaning and disinfection are not interchangeable. Cleaning removes dirt and lowers germ counts. Disinfection is the critical second step that kills germs on surfaces so the environment is safer to use.

In Dubai, shared spaces, frequent visitors, and long AC run-times mean touchpoints are used repeatedly throughout the day. Without structured disinfection, surfaces can stay contaminated even when they look clean, raising risk for families, staff, and guests.

  • Cleaning lowers germs, disinfection kills germs
  • High-touch points carry the highest risk
  • Wrong products do not kill the germs

    Yes. HomeFix supports apartments, villas, offices, retail spaces, and shared facilities across Dubai with trained disinfection crews.

  • What is the difference between cleaning, sanitizing, and disinfecting?

    Cleaning removes dirt. Sanitizing lowers germ counts. Disinfecting uses approved products to kill remaining germs on surfaces. Our service pairs cleaning prep with targeted disinfection.
