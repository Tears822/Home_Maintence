FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions – Annual Maintenance Contracts

Find answers to common questions about our AMC plans.

  • What does an Annual Maintenance Contract cover?

    HVAC, electrical, and plumbing servicing plus reactive callouts, helpdesk tracking, and emergency support aligned to your chosen plan.

  • What is the difference between emergency and standard response times?

    Emergency response is the fast-track dispatch time for urgent issues, while standard response covers routine requests. Higher tiers shorten both windows.

  • How many routine callouts are included?

    Each plan includes defined callout allowances—the first two hours are free, with additional time quoted transparently.

  • Do I get a dedicated support team under AMC?

    Yes. AMC clients access a dedicated helpdesk that knows your property profile, history, and priorities.

  • Are spare parts included in the contract?

    Parts are supplied as needed with prior approval. Preferred pricing and sourcing support apply to AMC customers.

  • What if the work I need isn't covered under the contract scope?

    Additional works are scoped, quoted, and scheduled through the same helpdesk with a 10% variation discount.
